About this area

Cairo

Located in Cairo, Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza is in the city center. Cairo Opera House and Gayer-Anderson Museum are cultural highlights, and travelers looking to shop may want to visit Khan el-Khalili and City Stars. Traveling with kids? Consider Giza Zoo and Giza Solar Boat Museum. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including outlet shopping.

About this property

Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza

Along with 5 restaurants, this hotel has 2 outdoor pools and an indoor pool. WiFi in public areas is free. Other amenities include 3 bars/lounges, a 24-hour fitness center, and a poolside bar. All 365 individually furnished rooms feature free WiFi and 24-hour room service. Guests can enjoy deep soaking tubs, and other standard amenities include DVD players, minibars, and LCD TVs.
